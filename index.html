Commanders TD - Documentation

Documentation of a Tower Defense Mode WarCraft III Map - Commanders TD

  • Authors: Jakub Gożuk, Patryk Madejski
  • Date of creation: 4-7 November, 2022

The map only seems to work on Warcraft III: The Frozen Throne.

Overview

Commanders TD is a tower defense mode for WarCraft III. The goal is to place towers that cost gold and give special abilities, heal, give damage and shield to the 10 units called "footman" that spawn from the castle and walk the maze to fight the other teams. Once the units exit your maze they will form up and prepare for a free for all death match. The last man standing will earn 3 points for themselves and the runner up will earn one. The first to the point goal wins.

You can place buildings, order units to move to the target area, order the units to stop whatever order they were previously given, hold position with the Builder that cannot be killed. It can also be used to patrol the area, focus the targeted unit with all towers in range or check the range of the selected tower. The player can change Builder's appearance to be an eagle, a penguin or a seal.

The game features a unique balance that allows for a large number of strategies ranging from high powered spell casters, single unit juggernauts, powerful armies, or death from above. This game values your ability to place your tower efficiently and micro what units they hit and how many times.
